This is mainly made for elementary students, so they can caculate easily.

How to use this calculator:

First prees any number of your choice, then press any operators of your choice, then press any number of your choice, then press the equal sign "=".

To delete something press "Del" button or the "AC" button.

DON'T type anything before pressing the "AC" button after once typed something else. DO NOT Press the "=" button before typing anything, so if you pressed it, then it will show "Undefined", so if you do press it, then press the "AC" button.
